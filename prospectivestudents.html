Opportunities for motivated B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. students passionate about Materials Science and Engineering.

Are you passionate about advancing the frontiers of Materials Science and Engineering?
If you are motivated, curious, and eager to work on innovative projects at the intersection of mechanics, metallurgy, and advanced manufacturing, we invite you to join our dynamic research group at IIT Delhi!

🚀Cutting-edge Research Work on projects involving additive manufacturing, high-entropy alloys, metallic glasses, and more.
🧠Supportive Environment Engage with a collaborative team, regular guidance, and opportunities to participate in conferences and workshops.
💼Career Development Gain hands-on experience, publish in reputed journals, and build your research network for a bright future.
⚙️State-of-the-art Facilities Access advanced laboratories and equipment to support your experimental and analytical work.

How to Apply

We welcome applications from motivated B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. aspirants. If you are interested in joining the lab:

  • Check ongoing research areas on the Research page.
  • Send your CV and a brief statement of purpose to labhead@email.com (replace with actual email).
  • For Ph.D./PG admissions, refer to the IIT Delhi Admissions Portal.
We encourage applications from all backgrounds and are committed to fostering a diverse academic environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What backgrounds do you consider?
    Students from Materials, Mechanical, Metallurgy, Physics, Chemistry, and related fields are welcome.
  • Can undergraduates join the lab?
    Yes! Highly motivated undergraduates can participate in short-term research projects or summer internships.
  • Is prior research experience mandatory?
    No, but a strong interest in research and willingness to learn are essential.
  • What should I include in my email?
    Please include your CV, academic transcripts, and a brief statement of your research interests and motivation.
