We’re always open to collaborating with highly motivated postdocs. If you're interested in applying your expertise to areas like fatigue, fracture, or creep in advanced materials, email us to discuss ideas and available opportunities. External fellowships are also encouraged.
Interested in doing an M.Tech or Ph.D. at IIT Delhi with our lab? You’re welcome to reach out! We love working with students who enjoy solving problems in structural materials and mechanics. Check the IIT Delhi admissions page to apply.
Undergraduate students at IIT Delhi interested in research projects (summer or semester-long) are encouraged to connect. Prior experience isn’t necessary — just curiosity and commitment. Reach out via email to start a conversation.
