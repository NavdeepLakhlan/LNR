Hi, I'm Lakshmi Narayan
I'm a researcher and teacher
I am passionate about advancing materials science and engineering through both research and education. My work spans metal additive manufacturing, microstructure engineering, and the mechanics of materials. I strive to bridge the gap between fundamental science and impactful engineering solutions.

Teaching

I'm Lakshmi — a passionate advocate for accessible and user-centered design. My journey weaves through academia, creative industries, and collaborative research, always with a focus on empowering others to do their best work.

Whether in the lab or online, I believe great design is both kind and bold: anticipating needs, removing friction, and empowering users of every background. I teach, I learn, I build, and I love sharing these experiences with students and collaborators.
