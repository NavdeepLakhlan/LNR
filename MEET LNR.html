Hi, I'm Lakshmi Narayan
I'm a researcher and teacher
I am passionate about advancing materials science and engineering through both research and education. My work spans metal additive manufacturing, microstructure engineering, and the mechanical behavior of advanced materials. I believe in making science accessible and exciting, and in empowering students and collaborators to reach their full potential. I enjoy solving complex problems and fostering a spirit of curiosity and inquiry in all my pursuits.

I'm Lakshmi — a passionate advocate for accessible and user-centered design. My journey weaves through academia, creative industries, and collaborative research, always with a focus on making technology intuitive for all.
