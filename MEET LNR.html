Meet Prof. Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian
Principal Investigator, Dhātu-yantra-pravartana Lab, IIT Delhi

About Prof. Lakshmi Narayan

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and received his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore in 2015.

Before joining IIT Delhi, he gained experience as a research associate at the Indian Institute of Science and as an Assistant Professor at IIT Jammu. His research interests include additive manufacturing, bulk metallic glasses, high entropy alloys, fatigue and fracture, and advanced characterization techniques.

Prof. Narayan has authored and co-authored numerous publications in reputed international journals, delivered invited talks at renowned conferences, and has been recognized for his contributions in the field of materials science and engineering. He is passionate about mentoring the next generation of researchers and collaborating widely on interdisciplinary problems.

Message from Prof. Narayan

"The journey of scientific discovery is as much about perseverance and curiosity as it is about expertise. At the Dhātu-yantra-pravartana Lab, we strive to push the boundaries of materials science through innovative research, team spirit, and a commitment to societal impact. I welcome collaborations and encourage students to join us in this exciting endeavor."
