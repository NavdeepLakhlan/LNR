Meet LNR
A Glimpse into the Mind and Journey of Prof. R. Lakshmi Narayan

Prof. R. Lakshmi Narayan

Hello! I'm Lakshmi — a curious mind with a passion for materials science, and a firm believer in learning by doing. My journey has taken me across academic institutions, industry collaborations, and around the world.

I currently serve as a faculty member at the Department of Materials Science and Engineering, IIT Delhi. I work at the exciting intersection of metal additive manufacturing, microstructure evolution, and materials characterization.

“To me, science is not about answers—it’s about asking the right questions, and daring to explore the unknown with integrity and imagination.”

My Teaching Philosophy

Teaching, to me, is more than delivering lectures—it's about mentorship, dialogue, and mutual growth. I strive to create a space where questions are welcomed, mistakes are seen as learning opportunities, and curiosity is the driving force.

Whether it’s in the lab or the classroom, I believe in learning by doing. I design courses and projects that bring theory to life—giving students the opportunity to build, break, analyze, and reflect.

What Drives Me

Every alloy has a story, every micrograph a message. I find joy in interpreting those stories—be it through fracture surfaces, diffusion gradients, or phase maps. My research is driven by a fascination with how the tiniest details shape the biggest outcomes.

I also deeply enjoy collaborative research—my work spans multiple countries, disciplines, and industries. I’ve been privileged to guide young researchers who have gone on to make significant contributions of their own.
