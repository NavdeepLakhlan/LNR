Dhātu Yantra

Prayogsālā
The group works on understanding the mechanical behavior at multiple length scales of advanced structural metallic materials, such as 3D printed metals and alloys, Metallic glasses, Superalloys, and High Entropy Alloys, using experimental, computational, and theoretical approaches. We strive to connect fundamental materials science with real-world applications and innovations.
