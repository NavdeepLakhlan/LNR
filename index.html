The group works on understanding the mechanical behavior at multiple length scales of advanced structural metallic materials, such as 3D printed metals and alloys, Metallic glasses, Superalloys, High Entropy Alloys, and other emerging metallic systems. The group is also interested in deformation physics, additive manufacturing, and structure-property relationships in these materials.
