Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and his PhD from IIT Bombay in 2014. He was awarded the Alexander von Humboldt postdoctoral fellowship to pursue research at the Institute of Materials Research, Helmholtz-Zentrum Geesthacht, Germany. He joined the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at IIT Delhi in 2017. His research interests include microstructural design of advanced structural alloys, deformation and fracture mechanisms, and additive manufacturing.
