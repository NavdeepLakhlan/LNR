Senior Research Fellow

J. Vigneshwaran

Senior Research Fellow (Dec 2023–Ongoing)

J. Vigneshwaran's research is focused on the preparation of complex metal oxides, activated carbon and 2D material synthesis, fabrication of electrodes, and electrolyte preparation for energy storage devices. Currently, he is working on the MXene and MXene-based polymer/metal oxide heterostructure electrodes for asymmetric supercapacitor coin cell or cylindrical cell assembly. He completed his Master of Physics from PSG College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore in 2014 and also received his MPhil in nanoscience and technology from PSG Institute of Advanced Studies, Coimbatore in 2016. He defended his Ph.D. in July 2024 at Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai, Tamilnadu and is a Research Fellow in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering (DMSE), IIT Delhi since December 2023.
