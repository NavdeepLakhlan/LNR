Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and obtained his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2015. He was a JSPS postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Materials Science, Tohoku University, Japan and an Alexander von Humboldt Fellow at the Technical University Darmstadt, Germany. His research interests include high-temperature materials, microstructure evolution, phase transformations and electron microscopy. He currently serves as an Assistant Professor at IIT Madras.
