Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and his Ph.D. in Materials Engineering at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, in 2015. He is the Principal Investigator (PI) of the group. His research focuses on advanced materials processing, structural materials, and the development of novel metallic systems. Dr. Ramasubramanian has published extensively and is dedicated to mentoring young researchers and students in the field.
