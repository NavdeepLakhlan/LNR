Principal Investigator (PI)

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

PhD – IISc Bangalore , B.Tech. – VNIT Nagpur

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and his PhD at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in 2013. His research interests focus on advanced materials, mechanical alloying and powder metallurgy, and high-temperature alloys.
