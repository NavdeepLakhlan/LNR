Principal Investigator (PI)

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

PhD – IISc Bangalore , B.Tech. – VNIT Nagpur

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and received his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in 2014. His research interests include advanced materials, mechanical behavior of materials, and nanotechnology.
