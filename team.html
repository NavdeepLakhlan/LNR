Principal Investigator (PI)

Prof. Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

PhD – IISc Bangalore, B.Tech. – VNIT Nagpur

Prof. Lakshmi Narayan completed his B.Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at VNIT Nagpur in 2008 and received his PhD from IISc Bangalore in 2014. He was a Postdoctoral Fellow at Carnegie Mellon (2015–2017), also working with Xi’an Jiaotong University. He received the NTU-SC3DP Fellowship in 2018 and joined IIT Delhi in 2019, currently serving as Associate Professor.

Post Doctoral Fellow

Dr. Ashwani Kumar

PhD – IIT Kanpur

Dr. Ashwani Kumar is a Postdoctoral Researcher at DMSE, IIT Delhi. His research focuses on ODS ferritic steels, duplex stainless steels, and AM metal matrix composites. He joined in Oct 2023 after his PhD from IIT Kanpur.
