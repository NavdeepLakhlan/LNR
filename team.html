Principal Investigator (PI)

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

PhD – IISc Bangalore , B.Tech. – VNIT Nagpur

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and his Ph.D. at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in 2015. He has held academic appointments at multiple research centers and his research interests include advanced functional materials, nanostructured oxides, and energy storage.
