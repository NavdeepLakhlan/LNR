Principal Investigator (PI)

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian

PhD – IISc Bangalore , B.Tech. – VNIT Nagpur

Lakshmi Narayan Ramasubramanian completed his B. Tech. in Metallurgical and Materials Science Engineering at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, in India in 2008 and received his PhD from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in 2014. He joined Carnegie Mellon University as a Postdoctoral Fellow in April 2015. During this time (2015-2017) he was also a visiting scientist in Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an, China. In August 2018, he received the Gopalakrishnan-NTU Presidential Postdoctoral Fellowship in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore and worked with the Singapore Centre for 3D printing (SC3DP). He joined the Department of Materials Science and Engineering, IIT Delhi in August 2019 as an Assistant Professor and is currently serving as Associate Professor. ORCID :0000-0002-6178-8124

Post Doctoral Fellow

Dr. Ashwani Kumar

PhD – IIT Kanpur

Dr. Ashwani Kumar’s research is focused on the synthesis, characterization and mechanical properties estimation of ODS (oxide dispersion strengthened) ferritic steels, duplex stainless steels and studying the tribological behavior of additively manufactured metal matrix composites. Currently, he is working on the mechanical behavior of super 304H steel and additively manufactured Al-Sc alloys. He completed his B. Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from UPTU, Lucknow in 2012, followed by M. Tech. in Materials Science from IIT Kanpur in 2015. He obtained his PhD in Materials Science and Engineering from IIT Kanpur in March 2023. He joined the Department of Materials Science and Engineering (DMSE), IIT Delhi as Postdoctoral researcher in October 2023.

Senior Research Fellow

J. Vigneshwaran

Senior Research Fellow (Dec 2023–Ongoing)

J. Vigneshwaran's research is focused on the preparation of complex metal oxides, activated carbon and 2D material synthesis, fabrication of electrodes, and electrolyte preparation for energy storage devices. Currently, he is working on the MXene and MXene-based polymer/metal oxide heterostructure electrodes for asymmetric supercapacitor coin cell or cylindrical cell assembly. He completed his Master of Physics from PSG College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore in 2014 and also received his MPhil in nanoscience and technology from PSG Institute of Advanced Studies, Coimbatore in 2016. He defended his Ph.D. in July 2024 at Madurai Kamaraj University, Madurai, Tamilnadu and is a Research Fellow the Department of Materials Science and Engineering (DMSE), IIT Delhi since December 2023.
